ABOUT ME
I am Noemi Castroviejo, a biotechnologist and bioimage analyst. I hold a PhD in Neuroscience and +8 years of experience in Molecular and Cell Biology laboratories from different fields.

Bioimage Analyst | Molecular Biologist

I have experience in developing and implementing analysis workflows
for cutting-edge imaging-based technologies, with a sound methodology for detection, segmentation, and classification. Proficient in using image analysis software such as ImageJ, ARIVIS 4D viewer, or Imaris, and experienced in analyzing and interpreting large datasets from light sheet and confocal microscopy.

Molecular Biologist with a background in Genetics

a great grounding in different skills such as: CRISPR screening, light sheet and confocal microscopy, DNA and RNA manipulation, cell culture, immunofluorescence, cloning, gene expression analysis, and cell sorting.

Data Science Learner

I am eager to apply my theoretical knowledge of machine learning to real-world problems in a fast-evolving environment, and I am also experienced in working in collaborative, cross-functional teams and contributing to interdisciplinary projects. My strong problem-solving skills and ability to manage multiple projects effectively, with attention to detail and a commitment to continuous improvement, make me a valuable asset to any organization.

This page is intended to provide you with more information about me and some of the projects that I have been working on.
